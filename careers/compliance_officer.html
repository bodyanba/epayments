Compliance Officer

We are looking for a Compliance Officer, who oversee the Corporate Compliance Program, functioning as an independent and objective body that reviews and evaluates compliance issues/concerns within the company. You will act as compliance ‘expert’ in a number of areas – Money Laundering, Fraud, Complaints and Data Protection.

Your responsibilities:
•

Develop and implement compliance Policies & Procedures

•

Enforce Policies & Procedures, analysis of reports, operations etc

•

Work with Operations Teams (Customer Support, On-boarding, Payments and Fraud) to handle day-to-day queries

•

Deliver training for new and existing employees, control of training Monitoring and carrying out assessment

•

Collaborate with auditors

•

Collaborate with partners

We require the following from candidates:
•

Experience of e-payments/financial technology

•

Experience of development, implementation and control of compliance Policies & Procedures

•

Experience of training the team in Policies & Procedures including monitoring and assessment

•

Deep knowledge and experience in international compliance: UK FCA and other regulatory bodies

•

Ability to interpret PRA and FCA consultations and regulations, and have a strong understanding of the PRA and FCA rulebooks, s as well as future developments

•

Managerial/deputy management experience in compliance space

•

Substantial decision-making ability, with a focus on helping the company achieve its strategic business objectives while managing the compliance risks involved

•

Strong analytical skills, business awareness and the ability to apply sound commercial judgement

•

Confident in assessing situations, operating autonomously and taking decisions

•

Flexibility to react quickly, reschedule and prioritise workload

•

Excellent knowledge of all relevant compliance and reporting requirements

•

Strong communication, interpersonal and relationship building skills

•

Highly organized and meticulous

•

Compensation package to be discussed on individual basis

For us the following candidate attributes are essential:
•

Economics/finance education, law, compliance

We offer our employees:
•

Competitive salary

•

Position in a large international company

•

Nice working environment: cozy modern office, friendly team and no dress code

•

A variety of cool tasks
